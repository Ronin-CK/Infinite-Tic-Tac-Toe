## âœ¨ Features

* **The "Rule of 3":** No more draws. Each player can only have **3 pieces** on the board at once. Placing a 4th piece causes your oldest one to disappear.
* **Modern Visuals:**
    * **Glassmorphism:** Translucent cards with background blur and noise textures.
    * **Dynamic Animations:** Floating background orbs, pulsing active pieces, and pop animations for new moves.
* **Smart Indicators:** The game highlights your oldest piece (the one about to vanish) by dimming it, helping you strategize your next move.
* **Lightweight:** Zero dependenciesâ€”just pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
